ABOUT

// RAOUL KRÄMER
// Who

I connect things that aren't supposed to connect. That's not a skill I learned — it's just how the brain runs.

Based in Hamburg. Working in the space between live experience, visual language, and whatever idea showed up at 2am that I couldn't shake.

By day I coordinate complex productions for Hamburg Ballett — managing the gap between what a director imagines and what actually fits on a stage in a city where nobody speaks your language. Before that, entertainment at sea. Before that, freelance. All of it: high stakes, fast decisions, zero tolerance for vague.

Parallel to all of this — drawing, photographing, designing. Not as a side hobby. As a second language. I think in images as naturally as in words, and I've stopped pretending those two things are separate.

I don't have a linear career. I have a body of work.
